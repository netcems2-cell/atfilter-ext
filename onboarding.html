What We Collect (Anonymous Only)

We DO collect:

  • Content keywords you block
  • Approximate region (city-level)
  • When filtering occurs (hourly bins)
  • Browser type & OS

We NEVER collect:

  • Your name or email
  • Specific URLs you visit
  • Page content you view
  • Passwords or form data

Why Community Participation Powers @Filter™

@Filter™ and @Map™ operate as a unified intelligence network. The filtering engine relies on the community sentiment feed to categorize content accurately. Your anonymous contributions keep the filter fresh and powerful for everyone.

Includes: Free, powerful content filtering + global visualizations with anonymous filtering categories (protected by differential privacy)

Your Control

You can change your mind at any time. Go to @Filter™ Settings to pause or resume community participation. Note: the filtering engine requires the community feed to function, so pausing participation will disconnect @Filter™ from the filter feed.

We understand!

@Filter™ requires the @Map™ community feed to power its filtering engine. Without participation, the extension cannot function.

If you're looking for a filter-only extension, you might also consider:

  • uBlock Origin — ad and content blocker
  • Privacy Badger — tracker blocker by EFF

You can always enable data sharing later in @Filter™ Settings.
